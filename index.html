What you'll learn about:


Shutter speed

Learn exactly how shutter speed works. You'll be able to control when you want to freeze motion in place or create slow shutter blur effects instead.


Aperture

Know what aperture is, what it does, and how to use it to achieve the look you want. You'll learn how to adjust your aperture to give you that beautiful depth of field that makes photos look so professional, all while still getting the perfect exposure.


ISO

Find out what is stands for and gain confidence in taking is off of auto! Learn the ISO "sweet spots" and how to adjust it for low-light shooting without getting too much grain.


White balance

Learn how to set white balance manually in any environment without using any automatic settings. Find out the secret to getting beautifully natural skin tones in camera without having to adjust them in post-processing.
